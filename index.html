About Me

Full Stack Developer!

I am a skilled web designer with over 1.5 years of experience in the industry. My passion lies in creating captivating website designs and implementing them through frontend development. I take pride in staying up-to-date with current design trends and leveraging my creativity to produce visually appealing and user-friendly websites. Throughout my career, I have developed a deep understanding of user experience (UX) and user interface (UI) principles. By putting myself in the shoes of the end-users, I strive to create intuitive and seamless browsing experiences. I believe that a well-designed website should not only look visually appealing but also provide a smooth and enjoyable interaction for visitors. When starting a new project, I thoroughly research and analyze the target audience and the client's specific requirements. This enables me to tailor my designs to meet their expectations and deliver a unique online presence that aligns with their brand identity.

Tri-Mode Arduino Rover Details

This project involves the development of a versatile Arduino-based car equipped with multiple control features, including obstacle avoidance, Bluetooth control, and voice commands. The car utilizes ultrasonic sensors to detect and navigate around obstacles, ensuring a smooth and collision-free operation. Additionally, it can be remotely controlled via Bluetooth, allowing users to maneuver the car using a smartphone app. The integration of voice control adds an extra layer of convenience, enabling hands-free operation through simple voice commands. This project demonstrates the seamless combination of hardware and software to create an intelligent and interactive robotic vehicle.

Virtual Health Care

Virtual Health Care is an innovative application designed to connect patients with a wide range of specialized doctors, including those focusing on teeth, eyes, general health, heart, and skin. Users can select their preferred doctors and related diseases using checkboxes or by typing in their symptoms or conditions. The application offers a seamless booking process and includes a secure payment method through Razorpay, ensuring convenience and security for users. This platform aims to provide accessible and efficient healthcare services from the comfort of your home.

Insure - Motor Insurance Made Simple

Insure is a user-centric platform designed to help individuals explore and secure the right life insurance plans effortlessly. The website simplifies the process of finding and comparing life insurance policies based on the user’s needs, ensuring financial protection for loved ones. Key Features: Instant Life Insurance Quotes: Users can quickly access quotes by providing basic information, ensuring they find the most suitable policies at competitive rates. Types of Insurance Plans: Offers detailed explanations of various insurance products, including term life, whole life, and universal life insurance, helping users make informed decisions. Why You Need Insurance: Educates visitors about the importance of life insurance for future financial stability and peace of mind. Easy Navigation: The website is built with a focus on user experience, providing a clean, intuitive interface that guides users through the process of selecting and purchasing insurance.

Insure Marketing - Your Partner in Smart Insurance Solutions

Insure Marketing is a dynamic platform dedicated to connecting individuals with tailored insurance solutions. Focusing on making insurance simple and accessible, it offers a range of services to help users find the right insurance policies for their needs, from health to life insurance. The website empowers users by providing a seamless experience for exploring, comparing, and securing insurance coverage. Key Features: Comprehensive Insurance Options: Provides a wide array of insurance services, including life, health, and business insurance, with easy-to-understand breakdowns of each. Smart Comparison Tool: Users can compare multiple insurance plans side-by-side, ensuring they make informed decisions quickly and easily. Expert Advice: The platform offers expert insights into the importance of different types of insurance and how to choose the right plan based on individual needs. Customer-Centric Design: Built with a focus on user experience, the website features an intuitive design that allows users to navigate effortlessly and find the right insurance solutions without hassle.
